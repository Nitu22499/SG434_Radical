About the system

Sikkim School Information System (SIS) is a UDISE+ compliant data management system. This system is tailored for the State of Sikkim to enable schools across 31 blocks within the four districts to record and share their statutory records effectively and efficiently with the higher authorities. This system could record data offline and sync them with other systems whenever the internet connection restores. This system will provide quality and credible data to the Government of Sikkim to help them effectively plan and structure their decision based on these data analysis.
